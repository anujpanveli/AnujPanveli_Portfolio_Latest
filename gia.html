Gia

Gia is an AI-powered digital finance assistant designed to transform enterprise finance operations, particularly within the order-to-cash (O2C) cycle. Functioning as a conversational AI—similar to Siri or Alexa but tailored for finance—Gia automates and streamlines various financial tasks, enhancing efficiency and customer engagement.

Gia operates 24/7, continuously learning from interactions to improve its performance and provide personalized assistance.​

Gia is equipped to handle a range of finance operations, including:

  • Automates credit checks, verifies business licenses, and retrieves credit scores from bureaus like D&B and Experian.
  • Generates and sends digital invoices, processes payments, and provides customers with multiple payment options.
  • Sends proactive payment reminders, collects dispute information, and schedules follow-up calls.
  • Matches payments to invoices by extracting remittance information from various sources, including emails and customer portals.
  • Responds to queries from both customers and internal staff, enhancing the overall customer experience.
