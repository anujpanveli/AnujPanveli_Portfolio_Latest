Visual

This project deals with a fitness-tracking and body-visualisation of an individual. The software provides modules such as 3D Body Visualisation, Workout Planning, Progress Tracking, Community Sharing, and Measurement Management. It allows users to create and view personalised 3D avatars that reflect their physical changes over time, follow home or gym workout plans, and track body metrics. The system is used by individuals looking to monitor and improve their fitness progress, and includes subscription-based access to advanced features.

This application has the ability to visually track fitness progress using a realistic 3D body avatar that updates with your measurements, making transformation easy to see and highly motivating

Key functions include:

  • 3D Body Visualisation
  • Progress Comparison Over Time
  • Workout Planning (Home & Gym)
  • Body Measurements & Stats Tracking
  • Community Sharing & Social Feed
  • Goal Setting & Transformation Tracking
  • Subscription & Account Management
  • Data Sync Across Devices
